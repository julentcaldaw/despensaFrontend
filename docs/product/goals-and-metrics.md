# Goals And Metrics

## Product goals (initial production phase)
- Deliver a complete and reliable pantry-to-shopping workflow.
- Reduce food waste caused by low inventory visibility.
- Improve speed of meal planning with internal recipe support.

## North-star metric
Weekly Active Pantry Households (WAPH): number of households with at least one pantry update and one shopping or recipe action in the same week.

## Supporting metrics
- Pantry freshness coverage: percentage of pantry items with updated quantity/status in the last 14 days.
- Shopping list usefulness: percentage of sessions where users complete at least one list action.
- Recipe activation rate: percentage of users opening a recipe after viewing pantry status.
- Duplicate purchase reduction: self-reported reduction over baseline after 30 days.

## Quality success criteria
- Core flows are end-to-end functional in production.
- Auth and role permissions are enforced in UI and API calls.
- Error states are understandable and recoverable.

## Review cadence
- Weekly review for operational metrics.
- Monthly review for behavior and retention trends.
