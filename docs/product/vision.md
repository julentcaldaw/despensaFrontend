# Product Vision

## One-line vision
Despensa helps people control pantry inventory, plan meals, and shop with less waste and less decision fatigue.

## Target users
- People who want to track what food they have at home.
- People who want faster shopping decisions.
- People who want recipe ideas based on available ingredients.

## Core problem
Most users do not know what they already have, what is about to expire, or what they should buy next. This causes duplicate purchases, food waste, and slow meal planning.

## Value proposition
- Keep a reliable pantry inventory.
- Convert pantry context into useful recipe suggestions.
- Maintain a practical shopping list with smart suggestions.

## Product scope (initial production release)
- Authentication and role-based access (`user`, `contributor`, `admin`).
- Pantry management (add, edit, consume, remove items).
- Recipe catalog from internal data.
- Shopping list management with manual actions plus automatic suggestions.
- Profile management.

## Non-goals for now
- Marketplace or grocery delivery integration.
- Nutrition tracking as a primary workflow.
- Multi-tenant enterprise administration.

## Long-term direction
Make Despensa a daily food operations assistant for households: from inventory confidence to planning and purchase execution.
